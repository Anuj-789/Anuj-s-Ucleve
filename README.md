## Problem
Many people have scrap at home, offices, or industries but:  
- **Don’t know where to sell it**  
- **Don’t have time to go and sell it**  
- **Throw scrap away carelessly**, which **increases pollution** and harms the environment  
- Traditional scrap collection is often **slow, inconvenient, and not eco-friendly**  

## How This Project Solves It
- **Doorstep scrap pickup:** Users can sell scrap **without leaving home**.  
- **Instant payment:** Quick and fair pricing.  
- **Accessible website:** Works on **mobile, tablet, and desktop**, includes **color-blind mode**.  
- **Chat via WhatsApp:** Users can directly **contact Ucleve** for queries or bookings.  
- **Eco-friendly:** Promotes recycling and reduces waste.  
- **Freely usable & community-driven:** Users can join the **Ucleve community**, follow on **Instagram/LinkedIn**, and give reviews via **email, call, or QR code**.
- 
**Project Idea:**  
This is my personal front-end project built for **Ucleve Traders**, a smart and trusted scrap trading company founded by Anuj Kumar. The website is designed to inform users about services, sustainability, and accessibility.  

The **main motive** of this platform is to help people **sell their scrap easily from their homes** without going anywhere. Ucleve provides **doorstep scrap pickup**, fair pricing, and instant payment—making the entire process simple and convenient for everyone.


## How I Developed It  
- Built the structure using **HTML**  
- Designed responsive layouts with **CSS**  
- Added interactive features using **JavaScript**  
- Implemented a **3‑tap Color Blind Accessibility Mode**
